COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a field that encompasses a wide range of topics related to computers, software, and hardware. It combines principles from both computer science and electrical engineering to design, develop, and maintain software and hardware systems.

ABOUT FACULTY

Computer Science Engineering faculty play a pivotal role in shaping the next generation of technologists and innovators. Comprising experienced professors,
researchers, and industry experts, the faculty members bring a wealth of knowledge and practical insights to the classroom. Their expertise spans a broad
spectrum of computer science disciplines, including algorithms, artificial intelligence, software engineering, networking, and more.
